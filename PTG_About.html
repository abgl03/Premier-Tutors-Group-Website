About Us

The Premier Story

Premier Tutors Group is a tutoring collective that aims to bring tutors and students together in a collaborative and social way. We are based in Auckland, New Zealand.

Our collective began with the goal of benefitting students and tutors alike. We act as a liaison between these two groups, allowing for lower costs to students, and helping tutors earn while they study.

Our Vision

Premier Tutors Group is not your average tutoring company. We are a collective, a team of tutors, proofreaders and directors who work together to provide our students with the best quality of service, and help them prepare for their next step.

It's not always clear which path is best, and sometimes we make mistakes. We believe strongly that the best way to prepare is to talk to someone with experience. With that in mind, we want to create a bridge from high school through to after graduation, with our working mentors and uni mentors ready to provide life experience and guidance through each step of the way.

Our Values

Premier Tutors Group works for our students, tutors and proofreaders. We will always do our best to make sure our students receive the best education and preparation, and our tutors and proofreaders get the support they need.

We also believe in social, inclusive learning, and we provide many opportunities to meet and socialise with other tutors and students.
